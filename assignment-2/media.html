About Me

Hello!
My name is Joel Hanson. I’m a GIS Enthusiast and Aspiring Backend Web Developer.
I have a background in Geoinformatics and Surveying, I’m passionate about exploring the world through spatial data. As a GIS enthusiast, I dive into location-based insights, crafting meaningful visual stories with tools like ArcGIS, QGIS and StoryMaps.
But I’m not stopping there — I’m expanding into web development to bring GIS to the web. From mastering HTML and CSS to diving into JavaScript, I’m building the skills to create dynamic, data-driven maps and applications accessible to everyone, everywhere.
Every project is a journey — whether I’m designing intuitive web pages, analyzing complex datasets, or simplifying spatial insights, I strive to make data speak clearly and beautifully.
Let’s build the future of mapping together!
